about me

Masters in Aerospace engineering at the University of Sapienza, Rome. Worked as Research Assistant in Physics Laboratory. Currently Teaching IGCSE Mathematics and Biology for IV Scientifico at Liceo Blaise Pascal

If you want to get the perfect grade in your AP final exam or you are looking for a better score for your calculus course, look no further. I can help you maximize the impact of your efforts and reach your goals.

I understand the student's pain point in the concepts, where he/she find it difficult or confused and build his/her concepts from there. Then as we do problems together, I find them with improved confidence and better equipped to solve the test questions. I find this technique to be most effective to improve the score.

I have previous experience of working with Math and Physics for 9 years and teaching students for two years. I've analyzed the collection of previous question papers (2007-16) in Trigonometry, Calculus, Probability to understand the pattern of the questions. This helps me to teach you the key points for scoring better with more ease. I can also share the questions with you if need be.

My experience in teaching IGCSE maths and biology at high school level will help explain in terms an Italian student can understand.
